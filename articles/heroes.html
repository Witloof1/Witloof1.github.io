My Computer Science Heroes

Over the course of my life, numerous people have influenced me and taught me valuable lessons—lessons that might teach you something too!

Daniel Shiffman

→Youtube

Daniel Shiffman helped me take my first baby steps in computer science land. He has a ton of tutorials, a lot of which using the p5.js library which you can use to create visually cool stuff with code. The tutorials go from making a ball move on screen to generating Perlin noise fields. He makes these tutorials with such a joie de vivre that you certaintly can't help but feel happier.

OneLoneCoder (Javidx9)

→Youtube

Javidx9 is a master at learning you how to code. He works on all sorts of projects, ranging from making his own game engine to making his own sound synthesizer. All of which he does in C++. I followed along with quite a few of his tutorials, learning a great deal along the way.
